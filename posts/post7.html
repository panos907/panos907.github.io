A single moment can change your life

13/07/2026

Yes, indeed.

It was 2019, in Essenspiel, at a project with Dragon Legion, when i discovered the game of Go. Previously, i had only heard of it or seen some static images of it, but never played or learned the rules. And there, i had the chance to learn, played a few games, and then played some more (on a beginner's board) with a good friend of mine.

Recently, i was cleaning off some stuff in my house, and came across a small travel-sized Go board i had bought on a whim. I dusted it off, and... i was hooked. And i truly do mean, hooked. I spent hours watching Youtube videos on theories and opening, attacking, defending, joseki, what is sente, what is gote, what is seki, so many things that i had never bothered to look up before while playing.

And then it hit me. I can keep doing this. This game has a depth unline any other, unlike Magic The Gathering, YugiOh, or other TCGs or board games i had grown to love. There was something there, a whole new world waiting to be discovered. On that simple wooden board, with black and white stones, i rediscovered my joy for learning and mastering a skill.

But this time, it felt different. Not so much a quick dive-in where i spend money and then the equipment sits idle while i do something else, no no no. This time, it was me changing my routine to revolve around Go, watching videos with my morning coffee, or before i head to bed, or meeting a local community and playing with them.
